Our Field Guide

What We built — proof, not template.

This is what We made for Ourselves using Functional Multiplicity. It is the field record from inside one System learning the practice. Read it for grounding, for examples, for the texture of what FM looks like when actually applied.

It is not a curriculum. It is not a class. It was built "in the field" of living life with DID — in the long stretch of figuring out what works and what doesn't. When You are ready to build Your own, see Your Field Guide.
