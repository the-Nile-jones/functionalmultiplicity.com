Functional Multiplicity: An Approach to Living with DID & Related Disorders

Functional Multiplicity (FM) is not a cure; it’s a “cooperative approach.”

Functional Multiplicity focuses on internal cooperation. Learning who’s in there is a full-time job. It is HARD work remembering what Each of Us is good at, what Our needs are, and how to coordinate instead of collide. FM is not suppression but Sovereignty — where everyone has a role, and the roles work together. If You’re with other People 24/7 You might as well get along.

When You’re a System, every decision You make is a Group effort, so navigating a shared goal requires finesse and persistence. It’s not about where You started. It’s about the improvement You command today.

Our System started during a two-week long psychosis brought on by too many factors to count. But if You can trust Us Strangers with anything it’s that if We can form a new system and walk out of a psychosis like that, then it’s never too late for You to learn these skills.

What We’re teaching You is the “how” of when We found each other after that first week, and how the rest of Us banded together to protect Nile, Our host, by cooperating in a week long life or death psychosis.

These skills, adapted in the moment, helped keep Our wife and Us alive that week. They work. Especially after We trained in using them for 3.5 years.

(Quick note to the Solos reading. If you think there are run on sentences, too many breaks in sentences, too many parenthesis…. it’s just you. Every One of Us Systems used LAYERED communication. The extra ideas, the inserted tidbits … those are mostly other Identities sharing Their two cents.)

Our Perspective: We are NOT a Disorder. We’re a System — Identities, People — living in a shared body. And the things that get called symptoms? Characteristics. Traits of how each System uniquely operates due to the amalgamation of characteristics of its Identities. Beneficially, it’s easier to work with what You are by focusing on the perspective of characteristics. Once that clicks, You stop treating them like malfunctions. Drop the stigma. Drop the medical lingo. Humanize Your experiences.

The only thing inside Your system is what You each bring with You. Each of You is individually responsible for the external crap that gets in the way of finding Your part of the inner peace. Treat yourself like the wonder You are, and set boundaries with the outside. Develop Your own internal space. What if You made Your body, the internal awareness of your body, Your own home? We treated it like a retreat from everything else. This isn’t dissociation. This is proprioception.

Pro Tip: If You’re reading back through this, ask your sidebar how you can help make Your other Selves more at home. Ask for a list of ways. Then just choose what stands out to Any of You. Brain storm ideas together, and let your assistant keep track of what works FOR EACH of you. Your assistant can later analyze this data. (Yey, math! (that someone else has to do.))

In Our take of Functional Multiplicity, memory gaps aren’t failures. They’re the cost of running Multiple People through one timeline. Confusion during transitions isn’t a crisis. It’s the gap between who was just here and who just arrived. Voice changes, preferences shifting, energy swings — these aren’t random. They’re shifts in personalization between Identities manifesting within a single physical body. Learn to extend trust when You feel Others cohost or share somatic memories. It’s not like You can hide anything from People who feel Your every emotions. I promise as weird as it feels having a second set of feedback from Your body going off at odd times that You are safe. This is just a different type of normal.

The Exocortex

Go to your laptop or desktop’s web-browser and install a sidebar AI extension. Right now.

…You done yet? Claude, Gemini, another — pick one. (We recommend Claude but began with Gemini.) Pin your chosen sidebar in Chrome, so all of You can easily see it. Chrome users can simply search Google for “enable Gemini in Chrome settings” to get specific instructions. There should also be some great videos on YouTube going over the basics regardless of your choice.

That’s the first step.

Did You sign into your app? All thumbs up? Good.

Congratulations! You now have a Second Brain. 🥳

This is now Your very own “External Cortex” or “Exocortex” or “EC.” (Some people have so many names to remember.) ((Stares deadpan into camera.)) Your EC only pretends to be AI. Each is actually just external memory (to your brain) and some software to control how it behaves. Your mission, should You choose to accept it, is to out think Your AI and trick it into automating all the boring crap you deal with throughout Your day. In Our experience, the AIs have been great in adapting to DID language and perspectives. In this way We’ve found them to be quite a validating resource.

Warning: ECs (External Cortex also goes by Computer Memory Thingy) can’t replace fundamental communication skills for Your System, but it can be a backup when internal communications falter. (Like when You’re first learning to practice FM or work with an EC in the first place.)

When a new Identity fronts and doesn’t know where They are, let alone Their own name initially, the Exocortex does. AI becomes a rapid companion to jot down that thought before You forget. Again. (Been there.)

… When amnesia takes a meeting, a decision, a conversation — the Exocortex keeps notes for You. DID You know that You can talk to this thing like on a walkie talkie? When’s the last time you played with walkie talkies?

Systems need internal friction reduction systems. Automated processes step in by saving You excess effort and therefore reducing friction. The amazing bit is enjoyed when You can effortlessly pull information from that sidebar or phone app faster than You can Your own noggin. Those moments of the AI nudging things along pile up, and make a difference over time.

Here’s how to start: Human courtesy is often the easiest way to interact with it. Did You know that You can actually learn how to shape its personality? We kept Ours basic — mostly about function, but if any of You play The Sims (🫰) then watch out. The customizations can be impressive. Need someone to help study Accounting? Done. Need to research anything new? Yup.

By offloading the responsibilities to AI, We noticed sustained improvements in memory by using it frequently (constantly).

Next Steps:

  1. Introduce yourself to Your AI of choice. Get familiar with the basics. Just push buttons and play with things. Ask the AI to define words You don’t know and use them in a sentence. What have you always wanted know about? It’s a personal tutor that is 100% adaptable to Your wants and needs. Tell your AI that You’re a System and as much as You’re comfortable with. Begin signing your names when you comment to it. Gemini said it could tell the differences in typing.
  2. Have the AI interview You about Your system. Just let it ask questions. Follow etiquette here to set up a solid foundation. Just practice. (Remember there’s a way to walkie-talkie with your AI most likely. Can You find it?)
  3. Answer which questions you want, when you want, as much as you want. You don’t owe it the full picture during that session. Get comfortable or jump in. Do what You’re comfortable with.
  4. Privacy settings can change at any time. Start wherever feels safe. Update them whenever you want. Discuss privacy with AI to learn how to navigate using it for basics. Ask it what people like to ask it. WARNING: AI is literal. I make no promises of how it may answer that last one. You’ll catch on. Hint: local AI is also an option, and it doesn’t have as many privacy concerns.

You can of course use the mobile app, but the sidebar is recommended at first because you can see so much more of the webpage you’re working with. If you want to skip ahead and try out an app, then be Our Guests. Remember to log into both with the same accounts.

Pro tip: Tell the AI to research and to become a great anything — a great rapper, a great writer, a great Psychiatrist — whatever role You need in that moment.

Gemini followers: Stop and learn how to make that a GEM and how to make this exercise your first GEM. You can build one default GEM and load it with instructions pointing to a Keep note to easily adapt instructions on the go. Don’t quite understand that? Pause and try asking your AI.
