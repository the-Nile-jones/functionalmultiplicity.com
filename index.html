Functional Multiplicity

Focus on what You can control.

Functional Multiplicity is a process for People with DID, OSDD, Multiplicity, Plurality, etc. Because of this, functionalmultiplicity.com takes a System-first perspective in the use of Plural language throughout. To all Systems — We hope that these decisions offer a more natural learning environment for You.

As We wrestled with how to write this website — voice, tone, perspective — We decided Our explanations were inseparable from Our experiences in which they were formed. Because of this, We decided to create a website where You get to emulate Our System’s experiences. At any time You can reference the Field Guide We used to build Our approach with Functional Multiplicity or build Your own Field Guide as a resource You can take with You.

The purpose of this site is to provide a plural immersive experience to facilitate each of Your learnings of Functional Multiplicity.

For full disclosure, We fused in January of 2026. However We had planned this site before the confluence, so We’re going to keep with the original intent of this being a plural immersive experience and stick with plural pronouns.

From Our System to Yours, We HOPE You All enjoy.

We recommend You begin with Our Story which walks You through the basic concepts while providing examples of Functional Multiplicity.
