Language for gender, identity, and plurality is still being built — by the communities who live it, not by institutions.

The resources below are peer-led references. They are starting points, not authorities. Your System's language for itself is the final word.

A note on terminology

This site uses terminology from The Plural Association and Power to the Plurals as its baseline. Both are plural-founded, community-led organisations. Their language is their authority. This site simply provides a starting point.

If Your System uses different language for itself, that language takes precedence — here and everywhere else.
